## FAQ

### How do I download a GoHighLevel lesson?

Open the lesson, classroom recording, or community post, click the GoHighLevel Downloader icon, choose the quality you want, and keep the tab open while the progress bar completes. The MP4 will save with the course and lesson name.

### Does it capture white-label or custom domains?

Yes. The extension recognises agency sub-domains, custom white-label URLs, and funnel paths so long as you have access to the content in your GoHighLevel account.

### Can I download entire courses at once?

Downloads are started one video at a time. You can queue multiple lessons, but there’s no batch export or ZIP bundling for full courses.

### Does it keep captions or transcripts?

The downloader focuses on video and audio. Captions, transcripts, and attachments aren’t included, so plan to keep those separately if you need them.

### Which browsers are supported?

Builds are available for Chrome, Edge, Firefox, Brave, and Opera on Windows, macOS, and Linux desktops. Safari and mobile browsers aren’t supported.

### Why does it request broad host permissions?

GoHighLevel frequently embeds third-party players and runs on custom domains. Host permissions cover those variations so the extension can detect and save every authorised video you can view.

### Is my data private?

Absolutely. Downloads happen inside your browser session and licence information stays on your machine. No passwords or viewing history are transmitted.
